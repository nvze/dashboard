Fondasi Manajemen Rantai Pasok

Definisi, metrik, dan strategi dasar
▼
1.1 Definisi dan Konsep Dasar
  • Definisi supply chain dan manajemen rantai pasok
  • Perbedaan supply chain vs logistik
  • Ruang lingkup: hulu ke hilir (upstream to downstream)
  • Pihak-pihak: pemasok, manufaktur, distributor, pengecer, pelanggan
  • Tiga aliran utama: material, informasi, dan keuangan
  • Value chain vs supply chain
1.2 Tujuan dan Metrik Kinerja
  • Tujuan utama: efisiensi dan responsivitas
  • Trade-off antara biaya dan layanan
  • KPI: On-Time Delivery, Perfect Order Rate, Inventory Turnover, Cash-to-Cash Cycle Time, Total Landed Cost
1.3 Strategi Rantai Pasok
  • Strategi efisien vs responsif
  • Push vs Pull system
  • MTS, MTO, ATO, ETO
  • Postponement strategy

Procurement & Supplier Management

Pengadaan strategis dan manajemen pemasok
▼
2.1 Pengadaan Strategis
  • Peran procurement dalam rantai pasok modern
  • Strategic sourcing vs tactical purchasing
  • Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)
  • Proses pengadaan: identifikasi → pemilihan → negosiasi → kontrak → evaluasi
  • e-Procurement dan platform digital
2.2 Manajemen Hubungan Pemasok
  • Supplier segmentation (Kraljic Matrix): Strategic, Leverage, Bottleneck, Non-critical
  • Supplier evaluation dan scorecard
  • SRM (Supplier Relationship Management)
  • Kemitraan strategis vs transaksional
  • Outsourcing dan offshoring decisions
2.3 Negosiasi dan Kontrak
  • Prinsip negosiasi dalam supply chain
  • Tipe kontrak: fixed price, cost-plus, incentive-based
  • Manajemen risiko kontrak
  • Incoterms (dasar)

Manufaktur & Operasi

Perencanaan produksi dan lean manufacturing
▼
3.1 Perencanaan Produksi
  • Perencanaan agregat (aggregate planning)
  • Master Production Schedule (MPS)
  • Material Requirements Planning (MRP & MRP II)
  • Capacity planning dan manajemen kapasitas
  • Bottleneck management (Theory of Constraints)
3.2 Lean Manufacturing & Just-in-Time
  • Filosofi lean dan eliminasi pemborosan (7 wastes)
  • Just-in-Time (JIT) production
  • Kanban system
  • Value Stream Mapping (VSM)
  • Continuous improvement (Kaizen)
3.3 Quality Management
  • Total Quality Management (TQM)
  • Six Sigma (konsep DMAIC)
  • Statistical Process Control (SPC)
  • Cost of Quality

Manajemen Persediaan

Model pengendalian dan klasifikasi persediaan
▼
4.1 Konsep Dasar Persediaan
  • Fungsi dan jenis: raw material, WIP, finished goods, MRO
  • Biaya persediaan: holding, ordering, shortage cost
  • Inventory turnover dan days of inventory
4.2 Model Pengendalian Persediaan
  • Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) dan variasinya
  • Reorder Point (ROP)
  • Safety stock dan service level
  • Periodic review (P-system) & Continuous review (Q-system)
  • Newsvendor model (single period)
4.3 Klasifikasi dan Segmentasi
  • ABC analysis (Pareto principle)
  • XYZ analysis (variabilitas permintaan)
  • FSN analysis (Fast, Slow, Non-moving)
  • VED analysis (Vital, Essential, Desirable)

Distribusi & Transportasi

Jaringan distribusi, transportasi, dan gudang
▼
5.1 Jaringan Distribusi
  • Saluran distribusi dan tingkatan
  • Direct vs indirect distribution
  • Cross-docking
  • Drop shipping
  • Omni-channel distribution
5.2 Manajemen Transportasi
  • Moda transportasi: darat, laut, udara, kereta, pipa
  • Intermodal dan multimodal transport
  • Routing dan scheduling
  • Freight forwarding dan 3PL/4PL
  • Incoterms (lanjutan)
5.3 Manajemen Gudang
  • Fungsi dan tipe gudang
  • Tata letak gudang (layout) dan slotting
  • Warehouse Management System (WMS)
  • Teknologi gudang: barcode, RFID, voice-picking
  • Material handling equipment

Perencanaan & Peramalan Permintaan

Demand management dan S&OP
▼
6.1 Konsep Demand Management
  • Demand planning vs demand forecasting
  • Demand sensing
  • Bullwhip effect: penyebab dan solusi
  • Collaborative Planning, Forecasting, and Replenishment (CPFR)
6.2 Metode Peramalan
  • Kualitatif: Delphi, market research, expert judgment
  • Kuantitatif: Moving average, exponential smoothing, ARIMA, regresi linear
  • Ukuran akurasi: MAD, MSE, MAPE
  • Pemilihan metode peramalan
6.3 Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP)
  • Proses S&OP bulanan
  • Integrasi demand, supply, dan finansial
  • Peran eksekutif dalam S&OP
  • S&OP vs IBP (Integrated Business Planning)

Manajemen Risiko & Keberlanjutan

Resiliensi dan green supply chain
▼
8.1 Risiko Rantai Pasok
  • Jenis risiko: operasional, finansial, geopolitik, alam
  • Supply chain mapping dan identifikasi titik rawan
  • Mitigasi: dual sourcing, buffer inventory, flexible capacity
  • Business Continuity Planning (BCP)
  • Supply chain resilience
8.2 Supply Chain Berkelanjutan
  • Triple bottom line: people, planet, profit
  • Green supply chain management
  • Circular economy dalam rantai pasok
  • Carbon footprint dan sustainability metrics
  • ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) dalam supply chain
  • Etika dan kepatuhan (compliance)
