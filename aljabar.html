Sifat-Sifat Bentuk Akar

Hubungan dasar akar dan pangkat pecahan:

$$ \sqrt[n]{a^m} = a^{\frac{m}{n}} $$
Perkalian
$$ \sqrt{a} \times \sqrt{b} = \sqrt{ab} $$
Pembagian
$$ \frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}} = \sqrt{\frac{a}{b}} $$
Akar Bersusun
$$ \sqrt[m]{\sqrt[n]{a}} = \sqrt[mn]{a} $$

Pemfaktoran & Aljabar Khusus

Bentuk Kuadrat (Pangkat 2)

$$ (a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2 $$ $$ (a - b)^2 = a^2 - 2ab + b^2 $$

PENTING (Selisih Dua Kuadrat):

$$ a^2 - b^2 = (a + b)(a - b) $$
$$ (a + b + c)^2 = a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + 2(ab+bc+ca) $$

Bentuk Kubik (Pangkat 3)

$$ (a \pm b)^3 = a^3 \pm 3a^2b + 3ab^2 \pm b^3 $$
$$ a^3 + b^3 = (a + b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) $$ $$ a^3 - b^3 = (a - b)(a^2 + ab + b^2) $$

Logaritma

Sifat-Sifat Utama

$$ {}^a\log(b \cdot c) = {}^a\log b + {}^a\log c $$
$$ {}^a\log\left(\frac{b}{c}\right) = {}^a\log b - {}^a\log c $$
$$ {}^a\log b^n = n \cdot {}^a\log b $$
$$ {}^a\log b = \frac{1}{{}^b\log a} $$
$$ {}^a\log b \cdot {}^b\log c = {}^a\log c $$
$$ a^{{}^a\log b} = b $$

Definisi

$$ {}^a\log b = c \iff a^c = b $$
Basis (a)
Numerus (b)
Hasil ($c$) 3
